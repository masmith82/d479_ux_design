Getting to Taniti

By plane...

Taniti International (TNI) handles two direct flights each week from major hubs. Seasonal charters operate during June, July, December, and January. Transfers to town take about 25 minutes.

By cruise

Cruise ships dock in Harbor District twice a week. Passengers can walk to restaurants and shops or book day excursions through local operators.

Airport Terminal

Small, modern, and easy to navigate.

Harbor Pier

Shops, luggage services, and ferry kiosks.

Getting Around Taniti

By car...

Rentals are available near the airport and downtown. Most island roads are paved and well-marked.

From $48/day

By shuttle...

Hotel shuttles run every 30 minutes between the harbor, beach resorts, and the main market.

Included with many stays

By bike...

Scenic bike routes connect Coral Bay to Old Town. Rentals are available at most hotels.

From $18/day
